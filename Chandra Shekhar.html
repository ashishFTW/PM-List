Chandra Shekhar (1927–2007)


Chandra Shekhar (1927–2007) was an Indian politician who served as the eighth Prime Minister of India. Chandra Shekhar was known for his commitment to socialist principles and his role in various political movements. His term as Prime Minister occurred during a period of political and economic challenges in India.

Early Life:

  • Chandra Shekhar was born on July 1, 1927, in Ibrahimpatti, Uttar Pradesh, India.

Political Entry:

  • Chandra Shekhar entered politics at a young age and was associated with socialist movements. He was a prominent leader of the socialist faction within the Indian National Congress before later joining the Janata Party.

Janata Party and Emergency:

  • Chandra Shekhar was a key figure during the anti-Emergency movement in the 1970s. He was arrested during the Emergency (1975–1977) imposed by then-Prime Minister Indira Gandhi.

Formation of Janata Dal:

  • Chandra Shekhar played a role in the formation of the Janata Dal in 1988, a political party that emerged from the merger of various opposition groups.

Prime Ministership:

  • Chandra Shekhar became the Prime Minister of India on November 10, 1990. His tenure as Prime Minister was relatively short-lived, lasting until June 21, 1991.

Economic Challenges:

  • Chandra Shekhar's government faced economic challenges, including a balance of payments crisis. He implemented economic reforms to address some of these challenges.

Relations with the USSR:

  • Chandra Shekhar's government saw changes in India's foreign policy, including a shift in relations with the Soviet Union.

Resignation:

  • Chandra Shekhar's government lost the vote of confidence in the Lok Sabha (lower house of Parliament) in March 1991. He resigned as Prime Minister, and his government was succeeded by a caretaker government led by P.V. Narasimha Rao.

Post-Prime Ministerial Career:

  • After his term as Prime Minister, Chandra Shekhar continued to be active in politics. He served as a Member of Parliament until his death.

Death:

  • Chandra Shekhar passed away on July 8, 2007, in New Delhi, India.
